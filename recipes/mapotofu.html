Mapo Tofu

Tofu marinated in spicy hot sauce mixed with ground beef. Best served with rice

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Tofu
  • Half lb Ground Beef
  • 3 stalks Scallion
  • 1 tsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 Tbsp Doubanjiang Sauce
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 tsp chili pepper (add more on preference)
  • 2 Tbsp cornstarch

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef. When it is almost done, add Doubanjiang sauce, sliced garlic, and chili pepper. Cook for 1 minute.
  2. Add chicken powder to 1 cup hot water. Add to ground beef along with soy sauce. Wait 1 minute.
  3. Add cut tofu and cook on high heat for 5 minutes.
  4. Add cornstarch to 1/4 cup hot water. Add slowly to the food until thick.
  5. Serve over hot rice.
